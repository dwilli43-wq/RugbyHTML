Join ND Women's Rugby

Interested in playing rugby at Notre Dame? ND Women's Rugby welcomes YOU!

Who Can Join?

• Anyone; no prior experience or body type required
• Open to all majors and class years (St. Mary's & Holy Cross included)

How To Join?

• Visit our Official Website for additional information on practices, location, and contact information
• Just show up to the pitch!

Why Join?

• Build lifelong friendships & stay active
• Travel for tournaments & take a break from homework
• Be part of a strong team culture
