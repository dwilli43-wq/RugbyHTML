Rugby Basics

Learn the fundamentals of rugby before stepping on the field!

Visit USA Rugby Video! 🎥

What is Rugby?

Rugby is a mix of football, soccer, and wrestling with unique gameplay mechanics and rules. It is a full-contact sport that involves running, passing, or kicking an oval ball to score "tries" against opposing teams.

Types of Rugby

Traditional (15v15)

  • 15 players per team | 40-minute halves
  • Structured positions (forwards and backs)

Sevens (7v7)

  • 7 players per team | 7-minute halves
  • Faster pace and more scoring

Basic Rules

  • Ball can only be passed backward or sideways.
  • Kicking forward is allowed.
  • Ruck: Ball is on ground; teams push to win it.
  • Maul: Ball is off ground; player is held up but moving.

Scoring System

Try 5 pts
Conversion 2 pts
Penalty/Drop Goal 3 pts

Player Positions

Forwards (1-8)

Bigger, stronger. Focus on scrums and rucks.

Backs (9-15)

Faster, agile. Focus on running and scoring.

Basic Gear & Equipment

Clothing (Shirt, Shorts, Socks) • Oval Ball • Cleats • Mouthguard • Optional Scrum Cap
