About Me

Hello, my name is Da'Sharia Williams. I am a junior at the University of Notre Dame majoring in Accountancy with a minor in Gender Studies. Before college, I played high school basketball and have loved sports since the age of 9. My high school did not offer many sports beyond the basics, so coming to Notre Dame encouraged me to explore new athletic opportunities. Since being here, I have participated in intramural basketball, flag football, and club rugby. This website focuses specifically on rugby. If you want to learn more about the sport, this is the perfect place for you.

During my freshman fall semester, I attended the same fair and this time I encountered some amazing teammates who really gave me a convincing pitch. I decided it was time for a change, so I followed through and attended the rookie day. At rookie day, we went through the basics of rugby and did tons of team bonding activities. It was at this point when I realized this was something I wanted to do, I felt a true sense of belonging. Since that point, I've continued to play and become better, working my way up to the A-team my first semester playing! Being a club team means we can travel-- mainly midwest universities such as University of Michigan and Ohio State University-- and in April 2025 I got to experience national playoffs in Washington D.C. which was AMAZING. All this to say, don't be afraid to try something new, it is worth the experience and honestly I am still learning each and every practice and game!
