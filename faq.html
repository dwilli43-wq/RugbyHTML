Frequently Asked Questions

1. Are there tryouts?

No, any experience level is welcomed. However, we do have a rookie day at the beginning of the fall semester and continuous developmental skills practices during the season!

2. Do I have to pay?

Yes, just like any other ND Club you must pay 'dues'. Typically ranging from $100-$150, and National Collegiate Rugby membership is about $100. Accomodations may be available for those in need.

3. Is this a large committment?

It depends on how serious you are. We have two practices a week (based upon weather),team workout groups twice a week (at least 30 min sessions) and and one game/tournament a few weekends out of the season (typically Saturday or Sunday)

4. How do I know I will get playing time?

Everyone gets playing time. We often have two teams who both play in games/tournaments making playing time very likely.

5. What kind of things does the team do outside of practice and games?

We love going out to eat, playing other sports, watching rugby games (or other films); Really, any team bonding is always an option!

6. Can I purchase merch?

The merch store is typically open during the spring season for a few weeks. In this period you can purchase any merch available. Once dues are paid, you also get a free rugby warm up shirt!
