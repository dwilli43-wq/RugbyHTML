Rugby Gameplay Tips

Learn techniques, strategies, and tips to improve your rugby game, whether you're a beginner or looking to level up.

Watch Rugby Skills! 🏉

Basic Skills

Passing

  • Always pass backward or sideways
  • Use both hands to guide the ball
  • Practice quick and accurate passes

Catching / Handling

  • Keep your eyes on the ball
  • Secure the ball when going into contact
  • Practice catching while moving

Tackling

  • Tackle safely with shoulder contact; avoid high tackles
  • Wrap your arms and finish the tackle
  • Stay on your feet whenever possible

Game Awareness & Strategy

  • Always know where the ball and teammates are
  • Support teammates in rucks and mauls
  • Communicate constantly on the field
  • Watch gaps in the opponent’s defense

Fitness & Conditioning

  • Train for speed, agility, and endurance
  • Strength training helps with tackling and scrums
  • Always warm up and stretch to prevent injuries

Game Mindset

  • Don't be intimidated by size differences
  • Stay calm under pressure
  • Have fun and enjoy playing rugby!
